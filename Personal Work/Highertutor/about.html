About This Website

This website is one where you can find qualified teachers and tutors of all subjects. The cyclings area on the Home Page makes it easy for you to find the subjects you (or YOUR CHILD) need(s) help with. The Register Page is where you can sign up for Highertutor. Highertutor's nice UI(user interface) makes it easy for you to find THE best teachers and tutors for yourself or your child. The aim of this website is to make sure that You (or your child(ren) if you have any) have the best tutors to keep your education up and going, anytime, anywhere.

How this webpage came to be

Divine Eddy, the creator and current owner of this website thought of this idea on the 26th of September, 2022. He made it his Goal to create websites that are beneficial to everyone. The name "Highertutor" was derived from the word "Hire (as in the act of employing someone)" and "Tutor(or teacher)", thus, the name "Highertutor" came to be.
